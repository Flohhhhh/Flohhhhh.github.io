Cameron is a student, artist, designer, and photographer working towards a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) focused in Graphic Design from Rutgers University. He is skilled with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Lightroom. Along with this, he has experience in a number of different digital mediums, including 3D modeling (Blender) and texturing (Substance), game development (Unreal Engine), and video editing (Premiere/Final Cut Pro).
